Oven Roasted Grape Tomatoes

Description

This is a wonderful, healthy side dish for summertime. Only make when the grape tomatoes are in season, their flavor is key! Great in the oven or on the grill.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb grape tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon chopped thyme
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Place tomatoes ont a large square of aluminum foil. Drizzle olive oil over tomatoes and top with garlic, basil, thyme, and salt. Wrap the foil around tomato mixture sealing tightly to keep juices inside.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until tomatoes are tender, about 30 minutes. Cool slightly.
